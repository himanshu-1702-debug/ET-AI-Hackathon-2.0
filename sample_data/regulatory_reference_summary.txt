REGULATORY REFERENCE SUMMARY (Plant Compliance Team Internal Notes)
Prepared as an internal quick-reference summary of applicable standards. For full legal text,
refer to the official Factory Act 1948 and OISD standards documents (linked in the compliance
document library).

FACTORY ACT 1948 - CHAPTER IV (SAFETY) - Key Points Relevant to This Plant:
- Employers must maintain effective safety provisions for hazardous processes, including
  proper fencing of machinery, pressure vessel safety, and hazardous substance handling.
- Confined space and hazardous area work requires documented precautions and supervision.
- The Act empowers factory inspectors to require documentation and inspect any part of the
  facility relevant to worker safety compliance.

OISD-STD-105 (WORK PERMIT SYSTEMS) - Internal Summary:
- All hot work, confined space entry, and other high-risk activities require a documented,
  time-bound permit with named authorizers.
- Permit issuers are expected to check for overlapping high-risk activities in the same or
  adjacent zones before authorization, since simultaneous hot work and confined space entry
  in proximity is a recognized high-risk combination.
- Gas testing before and during work is required for confined space entries in hydrocarbon
  processing areas.

OISD-GDN-178 (GUIDANCE ON SIMULTANEOUS OPERATIONS):
- Facilities are encouraged to maintain a simultaneous-operations (SIMOPS) register cross-
  referencing all active permits in a given zone during a shift, to catch dangerous overlaps
  before they occur, not just during post-incident review.
