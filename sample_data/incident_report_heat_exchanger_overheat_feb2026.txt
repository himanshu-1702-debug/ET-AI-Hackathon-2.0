INCIDENT REPORT
Report No: INC-2026-0019
Facility: Reference Industrial Plant, Zone A
Date: 2026-02-15
Reported by: Shift Team A (Day Shift), filed by V. Menon (Safety Officer)

Description: Outlet temperature on Heat Exchanger HE-33 exceeded the
high-temperature alarm threshold during normal operation. Unit was taken
offline per procedure and inspected. No injuries, no product release.

Findings: Inspection attributed the exceedance to fouling that had progressed
faster than the standard plant-wide cleaning interval assumes for this unit
(see maintenance log entry 2026-02-15 for full inspection findings).

Immediate Action: Unit cleaned and returned to service. Recommend a
unit-specific cleaning interval review for HE-33.

Classification: Incident, equipment parameter exceedance, no injury or release.
