Reported by: Shift Team A (Day Shift), filed by V. Menon (Safety Officer)

Description: Seepage observed at Valve V-88 seal interface, same location as a
similar event in March 2025 (see NM-2025-0041). Area isolated, maintenance
notified. No injuries, no significant product loss.

Immediate Action: Seal inspected and replaced (see maintenance log entry
2025-11-14).

Note from reporting officer: This is the second seal failure at the same
location on V-88 within eight months. Recommend a formal review of the seal
replacement interval for this valve rather than treating this as an isolated
event, since the recurrence suggests the current interval may not match the
valve's actual duty cycle.

Classification: Near-miss, mechanical seal degradation, recurring.
