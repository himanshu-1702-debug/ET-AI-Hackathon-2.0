NEAR-MISS REPORT
Report No: NM-2025-0067
Facility: Reference Spice Plant, Grinding Department
Date: 2025-05-19
Reported by: Grinding Shift Team, filed by T. Menon (Grinding Operator)

Description: Pigeon activity observed near the open feeding area close to the New Lithotech Grinder. Birds were seen perching on overhead structure above the grinding floor, an open-sided area with no netting or barrier. No confirmed product contamination, but the exposure is a direct food-safety and GMP concern given the proximity to open material feed points.

Immediate Action: Reported to Quality Control for awareness. No physical barrier installed at this time.

Classification: Near-miss, food safety / GMP — bird exposure.
